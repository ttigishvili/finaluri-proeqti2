COW

The cow has two horns, four legs and a long tail. A cow is herbivorous, leaves, and vegetables, and they are mammals. The most useful ingredient that a cow produces is milk. From the milk, we make different dairy products such as cheese, cream, sweets.

Donkey

A donkey is a four-legged animal that has 2 thin eyes, 2 thin ears, and a long tail. It has been used for carrying cargo for a long time. It can carry loads of great weight and quantity from one place to another without any problems, so this animal is often found in villages.

Horse

A horse is a four-legged animal with a long nose, two long ears and a long tail. The horse has been used for transportation for a long time. It is common in almost all countries and is quite expensive. Competitions are also often organized where horses compete, this is called horse racing.

Sheep

A sheep is also a four-legged animal with a long nose, short ears and a short tail. It has been widespread for a long time, it is widespread in almost all countries. We will often meet this animal in farms and fields. It is used as food, and yarn is made from its wool.

Wild Animals

Bear

Bears are mammals that belong to the family Ursidae. They can be as small as four feet long and about 60 pounds.

Lion

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large mammal of the Felidae (cat) family. Some large males weigh over 250 kg

Monkey

Monkeys are tree-dwelling (arboreal) simians. They are in the primate order. Monkeys are intelligent, social animals.

Tiger

The tiger (Panthera tigris) is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Panthera.

Exotic Animals

Capybara

Capybaras are short-haired brownish rodents with blunt snouts, short legs, small ears, and almost no tail.

Kangoroo

Kangaroos possess powerful hind legs, a long, strong tail, and small front legs. adult kangaroo weighs between 35 - 90 kg

Beaver

Beavers are among the largest living rodents in the world. They have thick fur, webbed feet and flattened, scale-covered tails.

Crocodile

Crocodiles have powerful jaws with many conical teeth and short legs. Males grow to a length of up to 6 m
